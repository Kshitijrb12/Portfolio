What kind of security testing services do we offer?

Asking us to provide the Penetration Testing, Security Audit and Vulnerability Assessment services for your web-application you can simply focus on your core business needs without ever having to worry about threats within your software.

TECHNICAL EXPERTISE

Our main focus is on web application security audits. SQLi and other sorts of injections, XSS (Cross Site Scripting), CSRF, Remote File Upload/Execution, and other types of vulnerabilities are all familiar to our expertise. We create reports that are completely free of false positives.

THE TEAM

To begin with, we are a group of security professionals. All of our pentesters are seasoned specialists with extensive expertise doing security assessments in a variety of cyber security sectors.

SECURITY TESTING SERVICE COST

If done properly, penetration testing cannot be inexpensive. However, because we are so certain that no online application is immune to attacks, we are always willing to operate on a pay-per-result basis, in which the customer only pays for vulnerabilities discovered.

TIME SCALE

Time is money, and we know how to operate under pressure. When doing penetration testing for web-applications before to public release, and when software is already live but existing vulnerabilities are being exploited by hackers, we are frequently confronted with both scenarios.

SUPPORT & ASSISTENCE

Dedicated professional teams are available online, via email, and by phone during the security assessment. The client will receive reports that not only illustrate but also explain the vulnerabilities discovered during the test, as well as tips on how to avoid such problems in the future.

CONFIDENTIALITY & ETHICS STANDARD

We recognise the importance of maintaining the security and integrity of our customers' data, thus we will, of course, sign the NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement). We will never make personal copies of a client's data, undertake unlawful testing, or share findings with third parties without their permission.
